About Us

Hadi Architect & Builder was established in Pattoki Punjab, Pakistan in 2022. The company grew organically and gained extensive experience in Pakistan. Currently giving services all over Pakistan, Hadi Architect & Builder has accumulated an impressive portfolio of projects that spans the breadth of building genres – from residential, commercial, leisure, retail, and interior design. The years of experience and absolute commitment to quality enhance the reputation as a premier contractor.
